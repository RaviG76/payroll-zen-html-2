Payment Details

How would your employee like to be paid? We recommend using our free Direct Deposit service to achieve paperless Zen, but you can also choose to write checks if you prefer. You can always change this later through the employee page.

By selecting Check as the payment method you will be required to write a physical check to this employee every payday (we will tell you the exact amount to pay). ZenPayroll will still calculate & withhold the appropriate taxes, like normal.

Be extra careful entering these values and make sure you include leading zeroes if they exist. We will verify the account by completing a test transaction. After adding this account you can split deposits between multiple accounts from the employee's payment methods section.
